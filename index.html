Pay-Per-Result Model

AIBS OS operates on a pay-per-result basis. Each payment of R75 / $4 / ₦6,500 purchases one AI-generated result from a specific tool. Because the result is generated and delivered immediately upon payment, all sales are generally final.

When We Will Issue a Refund

We will issue a full refund in the following circumstances:

  • You were charged but no result was generated due to a technical error on our side

  • The result generated was entirely unrelated to the inputs you provided

How to Request a Refund

Email us at hello@aibsos.com within 48 hours of your purchase. Include your payment reference number (provided by Paystack after payment) and a brief description of the issue. We will respond within 2 business days.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • You received your result but were not satisfied with the content — AI outputs vary and we cannot guarantee a specific outcome
  • You entered incorrect or insufficient information which affected the quality of the result
  • More than 48 hours have passed since the purchase
